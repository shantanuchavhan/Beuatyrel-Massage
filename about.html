About Rose Embroidery

Precision engineering meets artistic craftsmanship

Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the embroidery industry, Rose Embroidery Machines has grown to become a trusted name in precision embroidery technology. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide businesses and creators with machines that combine industrial-grade reliability with artistic precision.

Today, we serve customers across the globe, from small home-based businesses to large-scale industrial operations. Our commitment to innovation has led to the development of cutting-edge features like IoT connectivity, automatic thread detection, and cloud-based design management.

Every machine we produce is a testament to our dedication to quality, precision, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

To empower creators and businesses with world-class embroidery technology that transforms ideas into stunning reality. We are committed to continuous innovation, exceptional quality, and dedicated support that helps our customers achieve their creative and business goals.
