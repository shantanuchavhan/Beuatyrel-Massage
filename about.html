About Rose Embroidery

Embroidery machine sales • Expert service • Complete solutions

Trusted Embroidery Partners

Rose Embroidery Machines is a trusted provider of embroidery machine sales, service, and complete solutions. We supply high-quality machines along with expert repair, maintainence, and installation services to ensure smooth and efficient performance. Our offerings include Aari work, embroidery design samples, and customized embroidery services for businesses and individuals.

We take pride in our success by delivering reliable products and easy machine setup with clear guidelines, making it simple for clients to get started. Our strong maintainence support ensures long-lasting performance and minimal downtime. We have built a base of happy clients who trust our quality and service.

We also stay updated with the latest embroidery design trends, helping customers create impactful and high-selling designs. Our goal is to support creativity, improve productivity, and help our clients grow successfully in the embroidery business.

Our Mission: Empower Creativity, Elevate Business

To deliver unmatched embroidery solutions—from premium machine sales to precision maintainence—that fuel business growth, artistic expression, and long-term reliability.
