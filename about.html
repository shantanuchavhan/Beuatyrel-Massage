About RoseEmb

Our Story

Where Passion Meets Precision

RoseEmb was founded in 2012 by a team of engineers and embroidery artists who shared a vision: to create embroidery machines that combine industrial-grade precision with artistic freedom. Today, we're trusted by over 5,000 studios worldwide, from small home businesses to large-scale fashion manufacturers.

Every RoseEmb machine is rigorously tested to deliver perfection — from delicate silk monograms to dense 3D logos. We stand by our Quality Precision Trust promise with lifetime software updates and dedicated support.
