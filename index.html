Welcome to Rose Embroidery

Crafting Excellence Since 1995

With over 28 years of experience, Rose Embroidery has established itself as a premier manufacturer of precision embroidery machines. Our commitment to innovation, quality craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted choice for businesses across the globe. From small home-based studios to large-scale industrial operations, we provide embroidery solutions that deliver exceptional results every time.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility spans 50,000 sq ft and houses the latest CNC machining centers, ensuring every component meets our rigorous quality standards. We're proud to serve customers in over 30 countries with our award-winning machines.

Frequently Asked Questions

What warranty do you offer?

All our embroidery machines come with 3 years comprehensive warranty covering parts and labor. Extended warranty options available up to 5 years.

Do you provide installation?

Yes, we offer free installation and operator training with every machine purchase. Our technicians ensure proper setup and calibration.

What's the delivery time?

Standard delivery takes 7-10 business days for domestic orders. International orders typically arrive within 15-20 days. Express shipping available.

Do you finance machines?

Yes, we partner with leading financial institutions to offer EMI options with 0% interest for eligible customers. Leasing options also available.
