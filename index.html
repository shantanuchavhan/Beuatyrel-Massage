What types of embroidery machines do you offer?

We offer single-head, dual-head, and multi-head machines ranging from home studio models to industrial-grade production machines with up to 6 heads.

Yes! We provide one-day installation and training for all our machines, plus video call support for ongoing assistance.

All RoseEmb machines come with a 5-year warranty on core components and 1-year comprehensive coverage.

Absolutely! Our machines support cap embroidery, 3D puff designs, and even photo-to-embroidery conversion.
