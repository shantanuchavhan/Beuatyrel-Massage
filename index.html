About Us

Everything You Need to Know

Who We Are

Rose Embroidery is a globally recognized brand in embroidery innovation — fusing traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge engineering. With over a decade of experience, we empower entrepreneurs, designers, and large-scale manufacturers to achieve flawless embroidery outcomes through reliable technology and passionate service.

What We Sell

We offer a complete ecosystem of embroidery solutions: high-speed industrial embroidery machines, single-head & multi-head systems, Aari embroidery tools, magnetic hoops, digitizing tablets, specialty threads, needles, and maintenance kits. Everything to turn creative visions into stitched reality.

Why Choose Us

Uncompromising quality backed by ISO-certified manufacturing, 24/7 expert technical support, extensive training resources, and industry-leading warranty. Our machines deliver 1500+ SPM precision with energy efficiency and durability — trusted by thousands of businesses across 30+ countries.
